Biography:

Although I have been interested in pursuing this career since 2012, I had a vision that it was not for me and I should follow a more traditional career.

From 2016 I began to seriously see the possibility of entering this market, initially I just went to events and talked to local developers, after a while I started doing workshops and courses, watching GDC Talks and the like.

Among the areas of development, the one that most caught my attention was Game Design and it seemed to be the path I would like to follow. In 2018 I entered the IESB course on digital games, and really started making games, instead of staying only on the theoretical basis as before.

After countless prototypes and projects of very low quality I finally started to have successes, and after some friction with teams I decided to try my luck doing a project alone, it was a success, but because of its complications with deadlines and exams this project was abandoned , but the next one would be following the same line from the beginning.

This new and last project was Code: Bulwark Doll, a Puppet Control Castle Defense, it is currently in the process of being released and has been my best work so far.
